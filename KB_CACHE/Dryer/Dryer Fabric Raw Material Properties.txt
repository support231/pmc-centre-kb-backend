Dryer Fabric Raw Material Properties In Woven Dryer fabric, hydrolysis resistant polyester monofilaments are used to sustain hot and humid condition of paper machine dryer section for both Warp and Weft. Example: Warp PET Type: 910C, properties: Tenacity (CN/Tex) = 37; Elongation% at Break = 31%; Shrinkage % at 180 °C = 4% Weft PET Type: 900C, properties: Tenacity (CN/Tex) = 38; Elongation% at Break = 35%; Shrinkage % at 180 °C = 2%. In Spiral Dryer fabric, hydrolysis resistant polyester monofilaments are used to sustain hot and humid condition of paper machine dryer section for both Spiral and Pintle Wire. For Spiral High Shrinkage PET is used for better spiral formation and For Pintle Low shrinkage PET is used so that Siral Fabric do not undergo width shrinkage during running on Paper Machine. Example: Spiral PET Type: 930C, properties: Tenacity (CN/Tex) = 43; Elongation% at Break = 15%; Shrinkage % at 180 °C = 16% Pintle PET Type: 900C, properties: Tenacity (CN/Tex) = 37; Elongation% at Break = 34%; Shrinkage % at 180 °C = 2%.
