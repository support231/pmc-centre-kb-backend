Forming Fabric Raw Material Properties Forming: Warp Material: Polyester In forming fabric, high tenacity and high shrink polyester monofilaments are used for warp to get good fabric modulus and crimp for better seam strength and dimensional stability. Example: Warp PET Type: 921H; Tenacity (CN/Tex) = 63; Elongation% at Break = 13%; Shrinkage % at 180 °C = 11% Usage of 921H: Single Layer; 1.5 Layer; Multi-Layer-Double Layer, 2.5 Layer; SSB Bottom / Machine Side (MS) Warp Example of top / Paper Side (PS) warp type in SSB Fabric is 920Y for better smoothness of PS, this softer warp is used in SSB. Properties of 920Y: Tenacity (CN/Tex) = 57; Elongation% at Break =16%; Shrinkage % at 180 °C = 9% Usage of 920Y: Top side or Paper Side (PS) of SSB Fabric Weft Types: In forming fabric both polyester and polyamide are used alternately in weft. Polyamide PA 6 is generally used for better abrasion resistance. In Single Layer Low shrinkage polyester and polyamide are used. Example: PET Type: 900Y, properties: Tenacity (CN/Tex) = 40; Elongation% at Break = 40%; Shrinkage % at 180 °C = 2% PA Type: 804ZI, properties: Tenacity (CN/Tex) = 51; Elongation% at Break = 48%; Shrinkage % at 180 °C = 5% In 1.5 Layer Low shrinkage polyester and polyamide are used on PS and High Shrinkage PET & PA used in MS. For better edge curl control high shrinkage PET & PA are used at MS. Example: PS PET Type: 901Y, properties: Tenacity (CN/Tex) = 46; Elongation% at Break = 31%; Shrinkage % at 180 °C = 3% PS PA Type: 804ZI, properties: Tenacity (CN/Tex) = 51; Elongation% at Break = 48%; Shrinkage % at 180 °C = 5% MS PET Type: 913Y, properties: Tenacity (CN/Tex) = 48; Elongation% at Break = 27%; Shrinkage % at 180 °C = 11% MS PA Type: 814ZI, properties: Tenacity (CN/Tex) = 51; Elongation% at Break = 46%; Shrinkage % at 180 °C = 11% In Double Layer Low shrinkage polyester and polyamide are used on PS and High Shrinkage PET & PA used in MS. For better edge curl control high shrinkage PET & PA are used at MS. Example: PS PET Type: 901Y, properties: Tenacity (CN/Tex) = 46; Elongation% at Break = 31%; Shrinkage % at 180 °C = 3% PS PA Type: 804ZI, properties: Tenacity (CN/Tex) = 51; Elongation% at Break = 48%; Shrinkage % at 180 °C = 5% MS PET Type: 913Y, properties: Tenacity (CN/Tex) = 48; Elongation% at Break = 27%; Shrinkage % at 180 °C = 11% MS PA Type: 814ZI, properties: Tenacity (CN/Tex) = 51; Elongation% at Break = 46%; Shrinkage % at 180 °C = 11% In 2.5 Layer Low shrinkage polyester and polyamide are used on PS and High Shrinkage PET & PA used in MS. For better edge curl control high shrinkage PET & PA are used at MS. The extra PS weft called filler weft, is actually a warp yarn, gives extra fiber support and retention. Example: PS PET Type: 901Y, properties: Tenacity (CN/Tex) = 46; Elongation% at Break = 31%; Shrinkage % at 180 °C = 3% PS PA Type: 804ZI, properties: Tenacity (CN/Tex) = 51; Elongation% at Break = 48%; Shrinkage % at 180 °C = 5% PS Filler Weft Type: 921Y, Tenacity (CN/Tex) = 63; Elongation% at Break = 13%; Shrinkage % at 180 °C = 11% MS PET Type: 913Y, properties: Tenacity (CN/Tex) = 48; Elongation% at Break = 27%; Shrinkage % at 180 °C = 11% MS PA Type: 814ZI, properties: Tenacity (CN/Tex) = 51; Elongation% at Break = 46%; Shrinkage % at 180 °C = 11% In SSB Fabric Low shrinkage polyester is used on PS and High Shrinkage PET & PA used in MS. For better edge curl control high shrinkage PET & PA are used at MS. The binder weft, which binds the two layers of fabric together, is a high shrinkage PA weft, which prevents separation of two layers, called delamination, during running on paper Machine. Example: PS PET Type: 901Y, properties: Tenacity (CN/Tex) = 46; Elongation% at Break =31%; Shrinkage % at 180 °C = 3% PS Binder PA Type: 826ZI, properties: Tenacity (CN/Tex) = 61; Elongation% at Break = 31%; Shrinkage % at 180 °C = 13% MS PET Type: 913Y, properties: Tenacity (CN/Tex) = 48; Elongation% at Break = 27%; Shrinkage % at 180 °C = 11% MS PA Type: 814ZI, properties: Tenacity (CN/Tex) = 51; Elongation% at Break = 46%; Shrinkage % at 180 °C = 11%
